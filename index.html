📋 How It Works

👤 Step 1 — Sign In

Choose your name from the list, then enter your student number and tap Sign In. Guest students will also enter their first name.

📚 Step 2 — Pick a Section

Choose Vocabulary, Comprehension, or Cloze. Questions are shuffled every time. Complete each section once, then all three sections unlock for a second attempt.

🔁 Step 3 — Attempts

You get two attempts for each section. After your first try on all three sections, each section will unlock for one more attempt. After two attempts a section is locked for good — only your teacher can unlock it.

📖 Reading Timer

When each question appears, the answer choices are locked for 12 seconds. Use that time to read the question and choices carefully before making your selection.

⏱️ 12 second read lock

⏳ Thinking Timer

After you confirm your answer, wait 6 seconds before moving on. This gives your brain time to process the feedback — right or wrong!

⏳ 6 second pause

⏱ Overall Timer

A timer runs the whole time. It is not a countdown — there is no time limit. It only tracks how long you take so your teacher can see your progress.
